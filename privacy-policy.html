Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 16, 2026

This application is an internal business tool used solely to synchronize financial transaction data between QuickBooks Online and Salesforce for internal record-keeping purposes. It is not available to the general public.

Information We Access

This application accesses QuickBooks Online data — specifically payment transaction records — through the Intuit QuickBooks Online API. This data is used exclusively to create and update internal Salesforce records.

How We Use Information

Data retrieved from QuickBooks Online is used only to populate internal Salesforce records. It is not sold, shared, or disclosed to any third parties.

Data Storage

No QuickBooks Online data is stored outside of Salesforce and the organization's internal systems. API credentials are stored securely as encrypted secrets and are never logged or exposed.

Data Retention

Data is retained in Salesforce in accordance with the organization's standard data retention policies.

Third-Party Services

This application uses the QuickBooks Online API (Intuit) and the Salesforce REST API. Use of these services is governed by their respective terms of service and privacy policies.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact the system administrator of this application.
